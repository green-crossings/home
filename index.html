Welcome to Green Crossings!

Our mission is to:

  • capture interconnectedness among societies, governments, corporations, and nonprofits, in order to
  • empower individuals to understand ecological actions, disruptors, and impacts, and
  • empower organizations to maximize their collaborations and reach, engaging without overwhelming via
  • a credible, curated information portal, with
  • a colorful, easy to filter, explorable knowledge graph, and
  • online tools for building supportive communities.

On this site you will find:

  • information about the project and the principal players,
  • an curated, resource library,
  • embedded notebooks, or links to notebooks, containing tools for exploring our network database, and
  • tools to help people and organizations make connections.
